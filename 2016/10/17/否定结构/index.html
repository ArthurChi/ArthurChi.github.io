否定结构

Posted on 2016-10-17 | Edited on 2016-11-22 | In English

部分否定和完全否定

集体词用于否定句, 部分否定
All that glitters is not gold
并非所有发光的东西都是金子

I don’t know all of them

我不认识他们全部

All the horses here cannot run fast

这里, 不是每一匹马都跑的很快

注意

every是集体词

否定转移

观点类

  1. think
  2. believe
  3. argue
  4. assume
  5. image

似乎类

  1. seem
  2. appear
  3. feel
  4. taste
  5. sound
  6. smell
  7. look

例句

It doesn’t seem that we can get the money back.

状语类否定转移 (重点)

例句

There is no sky in June so blue that it doesn’t point forward to a bluer

翻译

六月份的天空无论多蓝, 都能让人想到更蓝的天空

例句

There is no man in the world so wise that he will not make any mistake

例句

The contemporary phenomenon of car worship is to be explained not least by the sense of independence and freedom that ownership entails.

解析

  1. en-tail 使成为尾巴->使成为后续, 使成为结果
  2. be to do = should to do

翻译

当代崇尚汽车的现象, 完全可以被汽车所赋予的独立自由感加以解释.

双重否定

例句

With the introduction of electric computer, there is no complicated problem{ but can be solved in a few hours.}

解析

  1. n + but + v, but + v == 否定性的定语从句
  2. There is no man in china but knows the Great Wall. -> but == who | that doesn’t know.

翻译

随着电脑的引进, 任何问题在数小时内都可以被解决.

例句

One’s true feelings cannot but come through in what one says and does.

累积

cannot but do

翻译

人们的真实感受, 一定会在言行中流量

例句

The aim of (the repetitions) of (the same commercial) on television is nothing but to brainwash consumers into compulsive consumption.

翻译

在电视上商业广告的重复播出的目的是给消费者洗脑, 以迫使他们消费.

例句

The importance of studying English cannot be overemphasized.

分析

over, too, 代表否定

翻译

学习英文的重要性, 怎样强调都不为过.

例句

He would be the last man to do such foolish things.

翻译

他绝对不会做这样愚蠢的事情

例句

With the introduction of electric computer, there is no complicated problem but can be solved in a few hours.

解析

n but + v 该类型结构理解为修饰名词的一个否定性的定语从句.

翻译

随着电脑的引进, 任何复杂的问题在数小时之内都能被解决

例句

The importance of studying English cannot be overemphasized.

解析

can not | can hardly | can rarely | can never + enough | ~er | too | over-~ 可以组成双重否定结构, 表示强肯

翻译

怎样强调英语学习的重要性都不为过

特殊否定

例句

Hardly a week goes by without some advance in technology that would have seemed incredible 50 years ago.

分析

Hardly ,修饰动词时候, 放在句首才会倒装
n 短定于 | 长定于, 短定和n合译, 长的分出

翻译

几乎每周都有技术上的进步, 这在五十年前是不可想象的.

例句

Yet there are few people {who feel anything but a mild interest in the discoveries that are being made by scientists.}

翻译

几乎所有的人都会对科学家进行的发现感兴趣.
