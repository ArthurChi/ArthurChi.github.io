分裂结构

Posted on 2016-12-19 | Edited on 2016-12-27 | In English

分裂结构的分类

  1. s, …, v | 主谓分裂
  2. n…(修饰语) | 名饰分裂
  3. invite sb, …, to do | 搭配分裂
  4. vt…o | 动宾分裂
  5. 插入语
  6. 其他

例句

And we are so accustomed to reading almost every week newspaper reports (about new discoveries) (being made
by man) that we tend to take the progress and benefit of scientific research for granted

分析

动宾分裂, 因为宾语长
that => so

词汇

tend to 往往

翻译

我们几乎每周都会在报纸上读到人类正在进行的新发现的报纸报道. 对此, 我们已经如此习以为常, 以至于我们往往把科学研究带来的好处视作理所当然的

例句

The Victorians, realizing that the greatest happiness accorded to man is that (provided by) a happy marriage,
endeavoured to pretend that all their marriages were happy.

分析

s,…,v
that 等价 the happiness

词汇

accord(vt) 等价 give

例句

The present question is that many people consider (impossible) {what is really possible if effort is made}

分析

consider n impossible, 但由于n太长, 所以将impossible前置. consider n impossible

翻译

当前的问题是, 许多人把如果付出努力完全有可能的事情视为不可能的.

例句

The sense is growing that the Americans need to turn things round fast, militarily and politically, if they are to ensure that events do not spin out control

词汇

grow vt种植 vi增长
spin 织网=>逐渐的

分析

growing在这里是不及物动词, 所以that引导的是同位语从句

翻译

这种感觉日益强烈: 如果美国人打算确保不逐渐失控的话, 他们就需要在军事和政治两方面, 快速的扭转情形

例句

A young man sees a sunset and, unable to understand or to express the emotion (it rouses in him), concludes that it must be the gateway to a world that lies beyond

分析

unable无法修饰conclude, 所以想到unable前省略了being, 这里作为原因状语.
连接词和连接主干的分裂.

翻译

年轻人看到日落, 由于不能理解也不能够表达出在他内心激发出的情感, 所以会认为, 它一定是通往天外世界的大门.
